The Challenge

The client, a premier global logistics provider, operated across 40+ countries using a patchwork of fragmented legacy systems. Each regional hub maintained its own siloed databases, leading to catastrophic visibility gaps.

Key pain points included high manual reconciliation errors between finance and warehouse departments, ballooning operational costs from redundant processes, and a dangerous lack of real-time supply chain data. This fragmentation resulted in frequent stockouts, lost shipments, and frustrated international fulfillment delays.

Primelabs developed a unified, custom ERP ecosystem designed specifically for multi-tenant global operations. We replaced the disconnected tools with a centralized cloud platform featuring three core integrated pillars:
