Platform

Overview
Business: Logistics & Delivery Technology
Product: Pariti
Service: Multi-Channel Delivery Marketplace (Web & Mobile)
Delivered by: PrimeLabs Business Solution
Pariti was designed to bridge the logistics gap between businesses, individuals, and
independent riders by creating a flexible delivery marketplace. The goal was to enable fast local
deliveries and nationwide shipping without requiring the platform to own riders or warehouses.
The Problem
Many businesses and individuals in Nigeria face persistent delivery challenges:
● Difficulty finding reliable riders quickly
● Lack of trust and verification in independent riders
● High and inflexible delivery pricing
● Limited visibility into rider availability nearby
● No real-time tracking of active deliveries
● Complex process for sending packages nationwide
● Small businesses unable to manage their own logistics fleet
● Poor coordination between local dispatch and inter-state waybill services
● Delivery companies without digital aggregation platforms
These gaps create delays, uncertainty, lost business opportunities, and reduced customer
satisfaction.
The Solution
PrimeLabs built Pariti, a smart logistics marketplace that connects senders, riders, and partner
delivery companies within one unified platform.
The system enables:
● Verified rider onboarding for safety and trust
● Real-time rider discovery based on location radius
● Flexible price acceptance model (similar to inDrive)
● Live rider tracking from pickup to delivery
● Local pickup and delivery workflow
● Nationwide waybill handling through partner logistics companies
● Optional last-mile doorstep delivery
Pariti acts as the coordination and visibility layer, allowing deliveries to scale efficiently
without owning physical fleets or warehouses.
Key Features Implemented
Verified Rider Registration
Riders onboard through a structured verification process where Pariti collects full identity and
operational details to improve security and trust.
Smart Rider Matching (Radius-Based)
When a sender creates a delivery request, the system automatically searches for nearby
available riders based on location radius.
Flexible Price Acceptance Model
Senders enter the amount they are willing to pay. Available riders can review and accept jobs
that match their preference — creating a fair, market-driven pricing system.
Real-Time Rider Tracking
Senders and businesses can track the rider live on the map from pickup to final delivery, similar
to the inDrive experience. This improves transparency, trust, and delivery confidence.
Real-Time Delivery Workflow
Once accepted:
● Rider receives job details
● Rider navigates to pickup
● Package is collected
● Delivery progress is tracked live
Nationwide Waybill Integration
For inter-state deliveries, Pariti partners with established logistics companies (e.g., GIG):
● Rider picks up the package
● Package is dropped at partner park
● Shipment moves to destination city
● Receiver chooses park pickup or doorstep delivery
Vendor & Business Friendly
Businesses can use Pariti to:
● Send orders to customers nationwide
● Manage multiple delivery requests
● Monitor shipment progress in real time
Trust & Transparency Layer
The platform builds confidence between vendors and riders through structured onboarding and
live tracking visibility.
Web & Mobile Experience
Optimized for:
● Rider mobile app
● Sender mobile/web booking
● Admin monitoring dashboard
Results
With Pariti, the delivery workflow becomes significantly more efficient and transparent:
● Faster access to nearby riders
● Live visibility into rider movement and delivery status
● Reduced delivery coordination stress for businesses
● Flexible and competitive delivery pricing
● Improved trust through verified rider onboarding
● Seamless bridge between local dispatch and nationwide shipping
● Scalable logistics model without owning fleets or warehouses
Pariti positions businesses and individuals to move goods faster, smarter, and with full delivery
visibility across Nigeria.
