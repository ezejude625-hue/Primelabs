The Solution

PrimeLabs implemented a custom Property Management System tailored to Nadice Real Estate’s operations. The platform centralizes property, tenant, and financial management while improving communication and transparency.

The system enables:

  • check_circle Tracking multiple properties and their units
  • check_circle Tenant management with portal access for complaints, payments, and documents
  • check_circle Expense and revenue tracking for each property
  • check_circle Payment scheduling, including installments for tenants
  • check_circle Automated reminders for lease expirations and payments
  • check_circle Easy access to agreements, receipts, and other property documents

This ensures property owners or agencies have a clear overview of revenue, expenses, and tenant activities, while tenants can interact and pay easily.
