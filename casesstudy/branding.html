Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process.

What is the typical timeline for a branding project? expand_more

Most branding projects are completed within 3 to 6 weeks, depending on the scope and number of deliverables.

Do you provide source files for graphic designs? expand_more

Yes, we provide all high-resolution source files (AI, PSD, EPS) along with print-ready and digital formats.

How long does it take to see results from Meta Ads? expand_more

While initial traffic starts immediately, we typically recommend a 2-4 week optimization period to gather data and refine targeting for the best ROI.

Can you manage our Google Business Profile monthly? expand_more

Yes, we offer ongoing management packages that include posting updates, responding to reviews, and local SEO optimization.

What do I need to provide for a promotional video? expand_more

We'll start with a discovery call. Generally, we need your brand assets, key messaging points, and any specific footage or products you want to feature. We handle the scripting, filming, and editing.
