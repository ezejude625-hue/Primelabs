help Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can we get started? expand_more
Onboarding typically takes 1–2 weeks from our initial discovery call. We review your business operations first, then configure and implement the right system for your workflow.
Do you provide ongoing support after setup? expand_more
Yes. All our systems include ongoing support, optimization, and monthly performance reviews. We don't disappear after delivery — we partner with you continuously.
Which system do I need — Growth or Control? expand_more
If you need more customers and visibility, a Growth System is the priority. If your operations are losing money through poor tracking, a Control System comes first. Many businesses benefit from both — we'll advise you during the discovery call.
What industries do you work with? expand_more
We work with gyms, retail stores, hotels, clinics, educational centers, and service-based businesses across Nigeria. If your business needs visibility, structure, or better data — we can help.
