Role Overview

As a Technical Project Manager at Primelabs Limited, you will play a pivotal role managing complex software development projects. You will act as the critical bridge between business requirements and technical execution, ensuring high-quality delivery within scope and timeline. You'll lead cross-functional teams to innovate and solve challenging problems in the fintech space.
