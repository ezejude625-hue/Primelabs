Role Overview

As a Senior Full Stack Engineer at Primelabs, you will be a key contributor to our core enterprise platforms. You will design, develop, and scale mission-critical applications that serve millions of users, working with a modern tech stack and a high-performing engineering team. We are looking for someone who balances technical excellence with a product-focused mindset.

task_alt Key Responsibilities

  • Architect and maintain scalable web applications using React, Node.js, and TypeScript.

  • Collaborate with product managers and designers to translate requirements into technical specifications.

  • Lead code reviews and mentor junior engineers to foster a culture of quality and growth.

  • Optimize application performance and ensure high availability across distributed systems.

  • Implement security best practices and data protection measures across the stack.
