info Role Overview

As a Product Designer at Primelabs, you will play a pivotal role in shaping our enterprise solutions. You'll bridge the gap between complex data requirements and intuitive user experiences, ensuring our B2B SaaS platform remains a market leader. You will own the design process from discovery through to high-fidelity implementation, working closely with product and engineering teams.
